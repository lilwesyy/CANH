Send 60 03 22 F1 50 to ECU 6F1

You'll get a response from 660
Send 60 02 22 XX XX to ECU 6F1

(the XX XX depends on the above response to get through the gateway - not sure about this yet)
You'll get a response from 660

Send 60 30 00 00 to 6F1

Receive from 660 XX XX XX XX YY YY YY YY

The YY's will be your odometer.

This isn't enough for you to get it done, but hopefully it will take you in the right direction. This above is from a 2015 BMW 328i. Good luck!
